Saint Louis University’s Office of International Services supports international students, scholars and employees, as well as students who wish to study abroad.

Our office can also help SLU departments navigate the process of bringing an international scholar or researcher to campus or employing an international student. 

We promote international student success and a global perspective for the University community. We look forward to enhancing your SLU experience.

Our Mission

The Office of International Services exists to promote the globalization efforts and mission of Saint Louis University and the Enrollment and Retention Management division. The Office of International Services supports the intercultural growth and global perspectives of all members of the University community through international student orientation services, cross-cultural events, global understandings, immigration compliance and international experiences.

Information for New International Students

Study Abroad

The Office of International Services has implemented new immigration software. Current students can request many items through SLU's international student portal. This includes signatures on the I-20 or DS-2019, driver's license or Social Security letters or CPT and OPT. Click on "Access the Portal" to the left of this paragraph. Log in with your regular SLU credentials. Select "forms" and then locate the item you need. Travel signatures are under the "reprint" option.
